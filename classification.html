🧪 Scientific Name & Classification

Use these short cards for your report.

They are Reptiles

Komodo dragons are reptiles, like other scaly animals.

Family: Monitor Lizards

Komodo dragons are part of the monitor lizard group.

Science Name

Varanus komodoensis

Communication

Smell, sound, and body moves help dragons talk.
