🍖 What They Eat

Use these short cards for your school project.

Big Hunter

Komodo dragons eat meat. They find food by watching and waiting.

Taste the Air

They stick out their tongue to taste smells in the air.

Strong Mouth

With a strong mouth, they can grab and bite their food.

Communication

They use body moves, smell, and sounds to share signals.

Fun! When a dragon uses its tongue, it can learn what is nearby.
