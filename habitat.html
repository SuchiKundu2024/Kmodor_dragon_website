🌍 Where They Live

Pick a card and copy it into your project.

On a Few Islands

Komodo dragons live on a few islands in warm places.

Warm + Dry

Their home is often hot and dry, with sandy ground.

Hide and Rest

They can hide in plants and rest in safe spots.

Communication

They use signals to tell others, “Hello” or “Stay away!”

Fun! Young dragons often stay higher up to stay safer.
